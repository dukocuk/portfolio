## üöÄ Entry points

Static browser SPA ‚Äî no CLI, API, or tests.

- **`index.html`** ‚Äî HTML shell; inline script sets `<html lang>` from
  `localStorage['lang']` before paint.
- **`src/main.tsx`** ‚Äî mounts `<App/>` under `<LanguageProvider>`.
- **`src/App.tsx`** ‚Äî section composition: Navbar ‚Üí Hero ‚Üí About ‚Üí Snapshot ‚Üí
  Projects ‚Üí Timeline ‚Üí Education ‚Üí Services ‚Üí Testimonials ‚Üí Philosophy ‚Üí
  Contact ‚Üí Footer (+ BackToTop, FirstVisitModal).
